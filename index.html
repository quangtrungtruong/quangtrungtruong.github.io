Bio

He is a third year PhD student at Hong Kong University of Science and Technology, advised by Prof. Sai Kit YEUNG and graduated with a B.Sc. at Ho Chi Minh University of Science- VNU. Before his PhD, he was a research assistant at SUTD. He collaborate closely with Prof. Duc Thanh Nguyen, Prof. Binh-Son Hua.
His research interests are machine learning and video understanding, particularly in video object segmentation, video editing and creation. Now, he has been working on knowledge distillation and diffusion models.
